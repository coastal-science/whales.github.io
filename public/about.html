About WHALES

Whale Habitat, Acoustic Learning, and Ecosystem Sustainability

Our Vision & Mission

At Whales, our mission is to foster collaboration and innovation in underwater acoustic research by connecting data, projects, and people across disciplines. We envision a future where open science, interdisciplinary methodology, and community engagement drive impactful scientific discovery in each of Canada's Three Oceans.
